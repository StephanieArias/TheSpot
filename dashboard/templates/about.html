The Inspiration Behind the Project

Stephanie and I both miss those immersive, sensational, & indulgent memories when we use to attend events pre-Covid-19. We decided from reminiscing that we're going to create a websites that enables users to search for events, things to do so that those lively moments can continue.

User can explore and discover events based on category

Users can look at and purchase tickets of events from upcoming week
